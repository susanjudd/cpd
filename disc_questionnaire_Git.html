Before you start

This is not a formal DiSC assessment. It's a practical insight tool designed to help you pay attention in your client meetings, by understanding yourself a little better first.

You'll be presented with 12 short scenarios from real adviser situations. For each one, choose the response that feels most natural to you. There are no right or wrong answers.

At the end, you'll get a personalised profile with specific watch-out moments for your client conversations, and a connection challenge to help you build your range as an adviser.

Most of us carry a combination of all four styles. Go with your first instinct. Don't overthink it. The result will point to your primary style, the one that tends to show up most in your work.
